We are AGS

 

We are 3 students from IPS and this is our game, a castlevania like game that follows the adventures of our brave knight in a quest to take back his castle, that was invaded by beings of another dimension

Characters

 

Claudio

One of the most powerful beings from the Ubuntu Realm, it now roams the realm of our brave knight bringing his minions with him

Knight

The knight of Finland is one of the strongest in his country and is faced with the hard task of taking back his castle from Claudio and all the minions from other dimensions

The Penguins

These cute little things are the weapons of the claudio, they are a double edged sword, for when they die, claudio suffers the pain of the penguins

Skeleton

The skeletons are the main grunts of the other dimension, brandishing a sword and shield they are capable of inflicting great damage

Jumping Zombie

The jumping zombie is the weakest of the invaders that our knight faces. Due to it's weakness and stupidity it is usually seen in packs of 2 or more
